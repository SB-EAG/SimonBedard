# SimonBedard — Simon Bedard's personal profile repository

This repository is the single, structured picture of who Simon Bedard is, what he
has built, and what he is working on right now. It is a profile and a map, not a
content warehouse. The full assets (the book, podcast episodes, marketing content,
the strategy model) live in other repos; this repo points to them.

## Read these first
When working in this repo, or whenever you need to understand Simon, read:
1. `profile.md` — who Simon is and how he works.
2. `body-of-work.md` — what he has built, with pointers to where each asset lives.
3. `current-focus.md` — what he is working on now.

## Keeping it current
`current-focus.md` is a living dashboard. Update it as workstreams change, and add
to `body-of-work.md` when something new is finished. Keep entries short and point
to the detailed record in the relevant project repo rather than duplicating it.

## Style and integrity
Australian English. No em dashes. Short paragraphs. Never invent facts about
Simon. If something is unknown, mark it "(to confirm)" rather than guessing.

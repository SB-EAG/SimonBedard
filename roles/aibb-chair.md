# Role: Chair of the AIBB

## What this note is

This is a signpost, not a strategy document.

It records that Simon holds the role of Chair of the Australian Institute of Business Brokers (AIBB), and points to where the detail lives. The Institute's strategy, governance thinking and priorities are held in the dedicated AIBB knowledge repository, not here.

This file exists so that anyone working in the Simon repository understands this role is part of how Simon spends his leadership time, without duplicating the Institute's material.

## The role in brief

Simon is Chair of the AIBB, Australia's peak professional association for business brokers and advisers involved in the transfer of privately owned businesses.

The role commits him to work well beyond governance, including:

- strategic leadership of the Institute;
- advocacy and government engagement;
- stakeholder and partnership relationships;
- conference leadership;
- industry representation and thought leadership.

## How this sits alongside his other roles

Simon wears several hats at once. Alongside the AIBB chairmanship he is:

- Founder and CEO of Exit Advisory Group;
- author of *Exit Like an Expert*;
- host and voice behind the Buy Grow Sell brand.

This matters for any future work about his time, energy, public positioning or how he is introduced. The chairmanship is a professional and reputational commitment, and it should be recognised as one input among several, not treated in isolation.
